No More Closed Doors

When administrators show up to the Senate, they come with promises they can't keep. They make decisions behind closed doors and rarely seek student input. The YCC shouldn't be a rubber stamp for admin PR. It should be a real channel of student power.

Here's what we'll actually do differently:

  • Publish private meeting notes with administration so you know what's really being said
  • Hold weekly town halls for students to voice concerns directly
  • Publicly break down the endowment so everyone understands where the money goes
  • Use the YCC's tools and reach to channel student frustration into organized action
  • Build democratic decision-making infrastructure that puts student voices at the center
  • Stand up for what we believe in as your reps, no matter the personal cost
